PRODUCTION WORKER QUICK GUIDE

Berjaya Autotech WMS | Panduan Ringkas Pekerja Pengeluaran
ENGLISH

1. Starting Your Shift

1 Log in with your Google account
2 Select "Production" role
3 Choose your assigned zone (1-23)

2. Scanning a Car (Zone 1 Only)

1 Select "Scan Car" action
2 Scan the car's VIN barcode
Note: Only Zone 1 can scan new cars. Zones 2-23 receive cars automatically.

3. Completing Your Work

1 Finish all tasks on the current car
2 Select "Complete Work" and confirm
3 Car automatically moves to next zone

4. Zone Status

WORK Processing a car BLOCK Waiting for next zone
STARVE Waiting for car PAUSED System OFF / Break

5. Break Times

Morning10:00 - 10:15Afternoon 115:00 - 15:15
Lunch12:00 - 12:20Afternoon 217:30 - 17:45
Lunch (Fri)12:30 - 14:30
Important: System automatically pauses during breaks. You cannot work during break times.
BAHASA MALAYSIA

1. Memulakan Syif Anda

1 Log masuk dengan akaun Google anda
2 Pilih peranan "Production"
3 Pilih zon yang ditetapkan (1-23)

2. Mengimbas Kereta (Zon 1 Sahaja)

1 Pilih tindakan "Scan Car"
2 Imbas kod bar VIN kereta
Nota: Hanya Zon 1 boleh imbas kereta baru. Zon 2-23 terima kereta secara automatik.

3. Menyiapkan Kerja Anda

1 Siapkan semua tugas pada kereta semasa
2 Pilih "Complete Work" dan sahkan
3 Kereta bergerak ke zon seterusnya secara automatik

4. Status Zon

WORK Memproses kereta BLOCK Menunggu zon seterusnya
STARVE Menunggu kereta PAUSED Sistem TUTUP / Rehat

5. Waktu Rehat

Pagi10:00 - 10:15Petang 115:00 - 15:15
T.Hari12:00 - 12:20Petang 217:30 - 17:45
T.Hari (Jumaat)12:30 - 14:30
Penting: Sistem berhenti secara automatik semasa rehat. Anda tidak boleh bekerja semasa waktu rehat.
