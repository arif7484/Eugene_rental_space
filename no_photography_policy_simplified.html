No Photography and Videography Policy

1. Purpose

This Policy ensures the privacy, security, and confidentiality of employees, visitors, and sensitive information within BERJAYA ASSEMBLY SDN BHD (BASB) premises are safeguarded and protected.

2. Scope

This Policy applies to all individuals entering BASB premises, including suppliers, contractors, customers, visitors, vendors, and any other individuals conducting business with the organization.

Prohibited devices include: Mobile phones, cameras, audio/visual recording equipment, and drones.

Applicable areas: All offices, meeting rooms, laboratories, manufacturing facilities, production areas, warehouses, and storage areas.

3. Policy Guidelines

3.1 Visitors & Business Associates

All visitors are strictly prohibited from taking photos, videos, or audio recordings. Business associates may only capture media under approved exceptions (see Section 4).

3.2 Auditors (Internal & External)

Auditors are not permitted to take any recordings. Media for audit records may only be permitted under approved exceptions.

3.3 Government Authorities

Authorities (DOSH, BOMBA, PDRM, Immigration, etc.) are generally prohibited from recording. BASB employees shall notify officers of this Policy and offer to provide necessary photos/videos upon request. Note: Care must be exercised under Section 186 of the Penal Code.

4. Exceptions

Exceptions may be granted for business, audit, security, inspections, or training purposes with prior written approval from management.

Visitors must submit a completed "No Photography & Videography Policy Request for Exceptions Form".

Conditions for approved exceptions:

  • Ownership: All media taken remains the property of BERJAYA ASSEMBLY SDN BHD
  • Confidentiality: Media must be treated as confidential and used only for the approved purpose
  • Non-Disclosure: No reproduction, distribution, publishing, or sharing on any media platform without prior written consent
  • Review Rights: BASB reserves the right to review any captured content
  • Indemnity: Visitors are solely responsible for claims arising from unauthorized use

5. Notification of Policy

The Policy is communicated through:

  • Hardcopy or softcopy provided via email
  • Signs displayed on premises
  • Excerpt on BASB visitors slip

By entering BASB premises, visitors are deemed to be aware of and agree to this Policy. A copy is maintained at the main guard house.

6. Employee Responsibility

Employees are responsible for enforcing this Policy in their areas. Any witnessed violations must be promptly reported to a superior.

7. Consequences of Breaches

If any visitor breaches this Policy, BASB reserves the right to:

  • Revoke permission to be on premises and request immediate departure
  • Request permanent deletion of all photos, videos, and audio from devices
  • Request retraction, correction, or apology if media has been published

These are in addition to any other contractual rights or remedies at law.

8. Update and Review

BERJAYA ASSEMBLY SDN BHD reserves the right to review and amend this Policy as needed.
